How to Play

🎯 Goal

Discover and collect all 25 unique cat breeds from around the world!

🐾 Exploring

Click "Explore for Cats" to venture into the wild and encounter cats. Each cat has unique stats and behaviors.

📊 Cat Stats

Cuteness: How adorable the cat is
Friendliness: How easily the cat trusts you
Energy: How active and playful the cat is
Intelligence: How clever and trainable the cat is
Rarity: How rare this breed is to find

💝 Caring for Cats

Choose the right action based on the cat's personality. High friendliness cats respond well to direct approach. Shy cats prefer observation first!

⚡ Energy System

Each exploration costs 10 energy. Your energy regenerates 1 point every 30 seconds, up to a maximum of 100.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Escape - Close modals
Enter or Space - Select focused cat card
Tab - Navigate between elements
